# Arahkaii Social Media Creator

Spin every Arahkaii article into a multi-platform social package that looks like one cohesive editorial brand — never AI-generated chaos.

## Step 0 — Load the foundation

1. `_shared/VOICE.md` (caption tone matches the article pillar)
2. `_shared/HALAL_SUBSTITUTIONS.md` (no alcohol copy, no alcohol imagery)
3. `_shared/IMAGE_SYSTEM.md` (palette + 24 image-prompt templates)
4. `references/social-templates.md` (the 10 fixed Canva/Figma templates)

## The 10 Locked Canva/Figma Templates

Never design from scratch. Every social asset starts from one of these:

| # | Template | Pillar / cluster default |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Instagram Carousel — Editorial / Listicle | Style · Dining · Guides |
| 2 | People Profile Carousel | People |
| 3 | Quote Post | Culture · Evening Edit |
| 4 | Reel Cover | All pillars (video promo) |
| 5 | Dining Guide Post | Dining |
| 6 | Travel / City Guide Carousel | Travel |
| 7 | Style / Fashion Post | Style |
| 8 | Newsletter Promo | Cross-pillar |
| 9 | Infographic Post | Evening Edit · Beauty (treatment explainers) · Wellness |
| 10 | Product / Partner Spotlight | Beauty · Living · Partnership content |

## Step 1 — Map the article to its default templates

For every article, produce:

| Asset | Required | Template |
|---|---|---|
| 1 IG hero carousel (8–10 slides) | ✅ | Pillar default from table above |
| 2–3 IG standalone posts | ✅ | Same template family or 3 (Quote) |
| 1 IG reel cover | ✅ | 4 |
| 1 IG story sequence (3 frames) | ✅ | Pillar default |
| 1 LinkedIn carousel | ✅ | 6 or 1 |
| 1 LinkedIn long-form post | ✅ | No template — caption only |
| 3–5 Pinterest pins | ✅ | 6 (vertical) |
| 1 Newsletter promo | ✅ | 8 |
| 1 Twitter/X thread (5–8 tweets) | optional | No template |

## Step 2 — Platform-specific voice calibration (loads VOICE.md)

| Platform | Voice | Length | Emoji | Hashtags |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Instagram** | Stylish friend sharing insider knowledge — voice of the article's pillar | Caption 1–3 sentences (or up to 2,200 chars for long-form) | 1–3 strategic, never decorative | 8–15 mixed-size, in first comment |
| **LinkedIn** | Industry editor sharing analysis | 600–1,300 chars optimal | 0–1, very minimal | 3–5, in-line at end |
| **Pinterest** | Trusted advisor guiding decisions | Descriptive but scannable, 200–500 chars | None in descriptions | None |
| **Twitter / X** | Sharp, witty, timely | 280 chars max | Strategic, platform-native | 0–2 |
| **Newsletter** | Editor's note — peer-to-peer | 80–120 words promo | None | None |

## Step 3 — Carousel structure (Template 1 — Editorial Listicle, 8–10 slides)

| Slide | Content | Voice rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hook headline (≤8 words) + small Arahkaii wordmark, swipe arrow | Pillar voice — declarative, never question |
| 2 | "Here is the short list" context (2 lines) | Service-journalism plain |
| 3–8 | One entry per slide: name + neighbourhood + one-line why + halal tag | Pillar voice |
| 9 | Editor's pick — one of the entries highlighted | Quiet authority |
| 10 | CTA — "Read the full edit on arahkaii.com / link in bio" + URL slug | Calm, no shouting |

## Step 5 — Quote Post (Template 3)

Single slide:
- Large serif type (Cormorant Garamond Italic per brand kit)
- Quote in 2–4 lines, max 20 words
- Attribution below: "[Name], [role]" or "— Arahkaii"
- Background: Paper or Warm Paper palette, plenty of negative space

Quotes pulled from the article — never invented. Never use a quote that contains a banned phrase.

## Step 6 — Caption writing rules

```
[Hook line — the article's argument in one line, pillar voice]

[2–3 sentence body — preview of value, name 1–2 specifics]

[CTA — soft, never shouty: "Read the full edit on arahkaii.com — link in bio."]

[8–15 hashtags in first comment, NOT in the caption itself]
```

## Hard rules

1. Never design from scratch — always one of the 10 locked templates.
2. Never use alcohol imagery, club lighting, or anything from IMAGE_SYSTEM.md §5.
3. Never use VOICE.md Tier-1 banned phrases in captions.
4. Never use generic engagement-bait CTAs ("Tag a friend who needs this!").
5. Never use ALL CAPS in body copy (only on small accent words inside designed slides).
6. Always credit the writer / founder / subject in profile posts.
7. Always include the article URL in the final CTA.
8. Always check halal status appears in Dining / Travel / Guides posts.
